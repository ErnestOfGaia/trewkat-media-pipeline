## ✅ Editor Checklist

### 1. Brand Voice Alignment
- [ ] **Plain Language:** Are technical terms (RAG, Regex, Mastra) explained or framed in a way a 55-year-old business owner in Tillamook would understand?
- [ ] **Honesty Check:** Does the copy avoid "hype" words like *revolutionary*, *cutting-edge*, or *10x*?
- [ ] **Tone Pillar:** Does it sound like a "local tradesperson" (warm and grounded) rather than a "corporate bot"?
- [ ] **Pronoun Check:** Are Ernest’s pronouns consistently **they/them**?

### 2. Technical Accuracy
- [ ] **Agent Roles:** Does the Librarian correctly point to `resume.ernestofgaia.xyz` and the coaching agents to `ernestofgaia.xyz`?
- [ ] **Stack Check:** Does the "Tech Specs" section correctly identify the use of **Mastra**, **Claude 4.5/4.6**, and **Voyage AI**?
- [ ] **Contact Info:** Is the phone number (503-664-0546) and email (eog@ernestofgaia.xyz) accurate in the social posts?

### 3. Platform Optimization
- [ ] **Medium:** Does the blog post feel long-form and informative?
- [ ] **LinkedIn:** Is the headline engaging and the formatting clean?
- [ ] **YouTube:** Does the description include the necessary links and a clear "Subscribe" CTA?
- [ ] **X.com:** Is the post concise (under 280 characters if possible) with high-impact emojis?

### 4. Grammar & Mechanics
- [ ] **Markdown Syntax:** Are headers, bolding, and links properly formatted for rendering?
- [ ] **Clarity:** Are there any run-on sentences that break the "neighborly" conversational flow?
